Expert Engine

  • Speculative Transcription

    We use Whisper-Turbo to process the first 10 minutes instantly. This allows the AI to start "thinking" about the subject while the rest of your 2-hour lecture is still uploading in parallel.

  • Contextual Overlap

    Chunks are stitched with a 3-second overlap. The AI uses the first segment's "Technical Glossary" to ensure accuracy across the entire transcript, preventing jargon errors.

  • 70B Intelligent Structuring

    The final pass uses LLaMA 3.3 70B—a model with massive reasoning capacity. It generates exhaustive summaries and concept maps that scale proportionally to the lecture's length.
